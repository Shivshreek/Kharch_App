About Kharch Tracker Pro

Learn more about our application

What is Kharch Tracker Pro?

Kharch Tracker Pro is a comprehensive expense management application that helps you track your spending, split bills with friends and family, and manage group expenses efficiently.

Key Features

  • Track personal and group expenses
  • Split bills equally or by custom amounts
  • Create and manage multiple groups
  • Real-time expense tracking and settlements
  • Visual statistics and reports
  • In-app messaging for group communication
  • Secure user authentication with OTP
  • Admin controls for user management

Our Mission

To simplify expense management and make bill splitting hassle-free for everyone, whether you're with friends, family, or colleagues.
